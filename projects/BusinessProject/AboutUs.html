Our Mission

To provide the most complete experience for students to find housing plans without extra stress. We as a company want to support the students of Michigan State University by keeping their focus on their academics, and to take another annoyance off the list of stressors for an average student at Michigan State University. We are in no part affiliated with Michigan State University but we are affiliated with every student, by being one.

Our Promise

Our promise is to bring the best option for choosing student housing to the East Lansing area. We will put forth the best product, and most recent reviews without diligence, to give students one less task on their list.
